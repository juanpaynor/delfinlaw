# **App Name**: Delfin Law Advocates

## Core Features:

- Practice Areas Overview: Comprehensive sections detailing the legal services offered, categorized for easy navigation and understanding.
- Attorney Profiles: Dedicated pages for each legal professional, showcasing their background, expertise, and direct contact information.
- Contact & Consultation Request: An interactive form for potential clients to inquire about services or schedule an initial consultation, along with firm's contact details and location.
- Legal Blog & News: A content section for sharing legal insights, news updates, and articles to demonstrate expertise and engage visitors.
- AI-Powered Legal Assistant: An AI tool that provides preliminary answers to common legal questions based on the firm's publicly available information and guides users to relevant practice areas or contact points.
- Client Testimonials & Case Studies: A curated display of positive client feedback and brief, anonymized case studies highlighting the firm's successful outcomes.
- Responsive Design & Navigation: A modern, intuitive navigation system and a fully responsive layout ensuring optimal viewing and interaction across all devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual identity draws from the logo's strong, classic palette. The primary color is a deep, authoritative forest green (#26734D), symbolizing stability and growth, used for main accents and interactive elements.
- The background features an almost-black hue with a subtle green tint (#111714), providing a sophisticated and elegant foundation for content.
- A rich, golden amber (#EBB447), inspired by the shell in the logo, serves as the accent color for call-to-action buttons, key highlights, and important information, ensuring contrast and prestige.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) to convey elegance, intellect, and a contemporary, professional feel. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, objective readability and neutrality, suitable for legal content.
- Icons should be refined and professional, echoing legal themes such as justice, balance, and stability. Subtle incorporation of shell or water motifs, respecting the 'Delfin' name, will add a unique brand touch.
- A clean, well-structured layout with ample whitespace to ensure high readability and a sense of formality. Content sections will feature clear hierarchy, with strong use of full-width hero sections and consistent card-based elements for practice areas and attorney profiles. Mobile responsiveness is paramount.
- Subtle and sophisticated animations, such as smooth transitions for section reveals and graceful hover effects on interactive elements, will enhance user experience without detracting from the firm's serious and trustworthy image.
